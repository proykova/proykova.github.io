No Perfect Memories

Film Details

  • Length:30'
  • Year:2021
  • Genre:Psychodrama
  • Language:Bulgarian
  • Country:Bulgaria

Synopsis

"No perfect memories" tells the stories of a blind tailor’s customers. Though the tailor cannot see, she is able to treat each customer’s childhood memories as separate albums and to put them back together into their souls. The customers all arrive with their own set of photographs but only some of the photographs are marked with uniqueness.
