Synopsis

It is a summer morning sunrise at the beach. A couple sit to enjoy the moment. The two notice a female figure in the distance. Within a few minutes each one of them creates a series of completely different ideas of who that person might be and how this person relates to them. The imaginations of the two will create two different imaginary worlds, provoking a variety of attitudes toward the figure in the distance.
